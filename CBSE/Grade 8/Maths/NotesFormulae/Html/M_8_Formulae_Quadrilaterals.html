Properties of all types of quadrilaterals (VISUALIZE and REMEMBER):
Shape
Sides
Angles
Diagonals
Rectangle
OPPOSITE sides are EQUAL
& PARALLEL.
ALL angles are equal to 90.
BOTH diagonals are equal
and BISECT each other.
Square
ALL sides are EQUAL.
OPPOSITE sides are
PARALLEL.
ALL angles are equals to
90.
BOTH diagonals are
EQUAL, BISECT, &
PERPENDICULAR to each
other.
Parallelogram
OPPOSITE sides are EQUAL
& PARALLEL.
OPPOSITE angles are equal.
ADJACENT angles sum =
180 (as they form co-interior
angles).
The diagonals are NOT
EQUAL, but they BISECT
each other.
Rhombus
ALL sides are EQUAL.
OPPOSITE sides are
PARALLEL.
OPPOSITE angles are equal.
ADJACENT angles sum =
180 (as they form co-
interior angles).
The diagonals are NOT
EQUAL, but they BISECT &
are PEPENDICULAR to
each other.
Trapezium
Only 1 pair of OPPOSITE
sides is PARALLEL.
2 pairs of adjacent angles
are supplementary.
(as they form co-interior
angles).
Nothing special about
diagonals.
Kite
Only 2 pairs of ADJACENT
sides are EQUAL.
One pair of OPPOSITE
angles are EQUAL.
The diagonals are NOT
EQUAL, but they are
PEPENDICULAR to each
other.
Isosceles Trapezium
Only 1 pair of OPPOSITE
sides is PARALLEL.
Other pair of OPPOSITE
sides is EQUAL.
2 pairs of adjacent angles
are supplementary.
Pairs of BASE & TOP angles
are EQUAL.
Diagonals are EQUAL.

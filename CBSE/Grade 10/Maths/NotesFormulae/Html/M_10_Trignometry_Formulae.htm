Relation between trigonometric ratios of complementary angles:

sin (90 – θ)  =  cos θ

cos (90 – θ)     =  sin θ

sec (90 – θ) =  cosec θ

cosec (90 – θ) =  sec θ

tan (90 – θ) =  cot θ

cot (90 – θ)     =  tan θ

Trigonometric identities:

sin2 θ + cos2 θ = 1, where  0 ≤ θ ≤ 90

sec2 θ = 1 + tan2 θ, where 0 ≤ θ < 90
